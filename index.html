About Us

Find out more about who we are and what we can do for you:

Viskantas Custom Landscapes is a young yet experienced landscaping company delivering quality landscape services to the Mississauga, Oakville, Burlington, Brampton and surrounding regions. We aim to help you see your property through to its full potential. We have the equipment and expertise to turn every landscaping idea into a reality and we will work with you along the way to ensure that every project is finished on time, on budget, and with a level of quality which will guarantee your satisfaction for years to come.

Services

A brief overview of the four primary categories our complete landscaping services fall under include:

Landscape Design

Before beginning any large scale project on your property, it is essential to design a plan which accurately describes the work to be performed. Having a plan of your property will minimize errors along the way and allow for work to efficiently and cost effective. With a well thought out landscape design, not all work has to be performed at the same time and you are able to space out large investments into your property over the course of several years. This allows you to make sure that the future work to completed in the future will be congruent with the work being performed in the present. We provide designs which incorporate hardscape features, softscape features, and maintenance schedules.

Learn More

Hardscape

Our hardscape services deal primarily large lasting installations on your property. We can perform excavation, grading, and drainage if you are not happy with the current condition of your land or we can perform beautiful stone work which will garner attention for many years to come. Our repertoire of landscaping abilities includes stone patios, walkouts, driveways, steps, retaining walls, and water features. We have experience using a wide variety of products such as interlock, flagstone, stone pool coping, natural and precast steps, and armour stone.

Learn More

Softscape

In addition to the hardscape work we provide softscape solutions to ensure your lawn and garden look their best. If you’re unhappy with the state of your lawn or currently don’t have any grass on your property, we professionally install our sod using quality top soil to make sure it grows healthy, green and weed free. We also offer flower, shrub, bush and tree planting in stunning arrangements which take sun, soil and other natural elements under consideration. Once installed, we seek to ensure that your lawn and plants grow healthy and maintain their vibrance.

Learn More

Maintenance

Our maintenance services provide reccuring treatments which will keep your property in pristine condition. We offer flexible packages which can include weekly, monthly or annual work maintaining your property. These services would include lawn care, garden bed mulching, tree pruning, weed control, spring cleanouts and snow removal. Our employees always maintain a professional and friendly attitude while keeping your property looking its very best.
