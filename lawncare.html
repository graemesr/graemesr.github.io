Sod & Lawncare

Are you unsatisfied with the current condition of your grass or do you currently have no lawn at all? Sod can provide an instant upgrade to your property and put an end to your lawn care problems for good.

Whether your yard is large or small, the state of your lawn makes a large impact on the overall appearance of your property. Sod can offer an immediate improvement and provide you with a lawn you can be proud of. However, it is important to understand the different types of sod and which installation techniques will provide you with the thick, healthy lawn you desire.

Viskantas Custom Landscapes has years of experience installing sod on large properties without any soil as well as replacing sections of grass which have previously died. Before the sod is laid, we level the existing ground and spread a quality top soil to provide a solid base for grass to grow. We use only the highest quality sod and top soil for our jobs to ensure that your lawn will grow dense and complete. If you’re in need of lawn care and want a new lawn today, contact us to find out how we can help you.

Our Process

  • Rip up existing grass
  • Grade the area to be sodded
  • Add a layer of topsoil and rake it flat
  • Lay sod in staggered rows
  • Cut out overlapping pieces
