Who We Are

Viskantas Custom Landscapes is a young yet experienced landscaping company delivering quality landscape services to the Mississauga, Oakville, Burlington, Brampton and surrounding regions. We aim to help you see your property through to its full potential. We have the equipment and expertise to turn every landscaping idea into a reality and we will work with you along the way to ensure that every project is finished on time, on budget, and with a level of quality which will guarantee your satisfaction for years to come. Our company provides landscaping services which can roughly be grouped into four main categories: Landscape design, hardscape, softscape, and maintenance.

History

After gaining many years of experience working at an established landscaping business, company founder Justin Viskantas came to the realization that it was time to branch out on his own. Like anyone attempting to start their own business, many obstacles presented themselves along the way in the beginning. However, after navigating the turbulent times early on, the company began to find its footing by providing top quality work for a fair price. We are proud to say that Viskantas Custom Landscapes has served many satisfied customers from all across the GTA and will continue to do so for many years to come.
